# Safo Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-05-14

This draft is intended as a working version for Safo before final legal review and publishing.

## 1. Overview

Safo helps users manage pantry items, shopping lists, meal plans, recipes, and shared household activity.

We aim to collect only the data needed to provide these features and improve the app experience.

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Safo, we may collect:

- account information such as email address
- household information such as household name and membership
- pantry data such as food items, quantities, storage location, expiration dates, and opened status
- shopping list data
- meal planning data
- recipe and dietary preference data
- allergy and intolerance preferences
- scan-related data such as barcode lookup results or fridge scan results
- basic technical information required for authentication, stability, and security

## 3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- provide core Safo functionality
- sync your pantry, shopping list, household, and meal plan
- personalize suggestions and warnings
- support household sharing features
- improve reliability and product quality
- protect the app against abuse or misuse

## 4. Household Sharing

If you join or create a shared household, information inside that household may be visible to other members of that same household. This includes shared pantry items, shopping list items, meal plans, and certain activity history.

## 5. Allergies, Intolerances, and Diet Preferences

Safo may use allergy, intolerance, and diet preference information to show warnings, safer suggestions, or alternative ingredient ideas.

This information is intended to support planning and awareness. It is not medical advice.

## 6. Photos and Camera Access

If you use fridge scanning or other photo-based features, Safo may request access to your camera or photo library. Images are used only to provide the requested scanning feature.

## 7. Authentication and Backend Services

Safo uses backend services to manage authentication, data storage, and synchronization. These services may process personal data on our behalf in order to operate the app.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account or household remains active, or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## 9. Your Choices

You may be able to:

- update your pantry, household, and profile-related information
- change dietary preferences and personal settings
- leave a household
- request account deletion if supported by the final production workflow

## 10. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect user information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Children

Safo is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law without supervision from a parent or guardian.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
