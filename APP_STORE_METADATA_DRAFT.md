## Long Description Draft

Safo is your shared kitchen assistant for everyday home life.

Know what you already have, what should be used soon, what is worth cooking today, and what still needs to be bought. Safo helps you keep your pantry organized, simplify meal planning, and make shopping easier for the whole household.

With Safo you can:

- track pantry items and expiration dates
- see what should be used soon
- plan meals based on what you already have
- add missing ingredients to the shopping list
- manage shopping together with your household
- save time when deciding what to cook
- avoid unnecessary food waste
- keep dietary preferences, allergies, and intolerances in mind

Safo is designed to feel like a practical kitchen assistant, not just a food database.
